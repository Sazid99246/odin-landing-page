Excellent steaks delivered to your door

Buying steaks just got a whole lot easier. The NS Steak Delivery Company offers hand cut steaks delivered by mail. Home delivery is our way of delivering the freshest and best cuts of meat to you as quickly as possible.
Steak types
The rib eye is a boneless rib steak from the rib section
Filet mignon is a cut of meat taken from the center and butt thirds of beef tenderloin
The T-bone and potterhouse are steaks of beef cut from the short loin.
The Tomahawk steak is an on-the-bone rib steak, cut from the fore-tib with the entire rib bone left
A mighty porterhouse steak an inch and a half thick, hot and sputtering from the griddle; dusted with fragnant pepper; enriched with little melting bits of butter of the most impeachable freshness and genuineness; the precious juices of the meat trickling out and joining the gravy, archipelagoed with mushrooms; a township or two of tender, yellowish fat gracing an out-lying district of this ample county of beefsteak; the long white bone which divides the sirloin from the tenderloin still in its place.
